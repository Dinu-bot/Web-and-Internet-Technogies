Vaccination Schedule

Keep your pets healthy and protected. Below are recommended vaccination schedules:

  • Puppies: Every 3–4 weeks until 16 weeks old
  • Kittens: Every 3–4 weeks until 16 weeks old
  • Annual boosters for adult pets

Benefits of Vaccination

Vaccinations help protect pets from common diseases, improve lifespan, and ensure community safety.

FAQs

Q: Are vaccinations safe?
A: Yes, they are essential and monitored by vets.

Q: Can my pet be vaccinated when sick?
A: It's best to wait until your pet is healthy.
